Older homes have character, but ageing plasterwork can let them down. Just Plaster specialises in restoring and refreshing existing plasterwork to its former glory.

## Restoration services

- Heritage plaster restoration
- Ornamental cornice repair and replication
- Cracked wall and ceiling restoration
- Water stain treatment and refinishing
- Textured finish restoration

## Respect for your home

We understand the value of period features and original character. Our restoration work preserves what makes your home special while bringing it up to modern standards.
